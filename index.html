About Our School

Founded in 2007, Shaykosh Boarding School is the first boarding institution established specifically for pastoralist children in the Somali Regional State.

Our mission is to provide quality residential education grounded in Islamic teachings, academic knowledge, and comprehensive care that prepares students to contribute to their communities.
